## 中文

我是 **蘇靖淵**,目前就讀 **國立陽明交通大學 智能系統研究所 (NYCU PAIRLabs)**,指導教授為曾煜棋老師。

I'm **Ching-Yuan Su**, an M.S. student at **NYCU Institute of Intelligent Systems (PAIRLabs)**, advised by Prof. Yu-Chee Tseng.

My research, **LLM Commander**, is an offline Chinese voice-to-UAV control pipeline — QLoRA-fine-tuned Gemma 3 translating natural language into MAVLink JSON, with ASR (RTX 3090) and LLM (RTX 4090) inference split across machines via Tailscale.

Previously: three-year web-dev internship at CCU Computer Center (WCAG 2.1 accessibility overhaul, PHP annual-report system), and Department Student Association President at CCU (2022–2023).
